Hospital Lift FAQs

What is the price of a hospital lift in India?

Hospital lift prices in India typically range from ₹8 Lakh to ₹20 Lakh depending on size, capacity, number of floors, and required features. Stretcher-compatible hospital elevators with full safety systems start around ₹10 Lakh. Contact Aerix for an exact quote.

What size does a hospital elevator need to be?

As per IS 14665 and international standards, a hospital elevator should have a minimum cabin size of 2100mm (depth) × 1100mm (width) to accommodate a standard hospital stretcher. Aerix designs hospital lifts to exact BIS and EN81-70 accessibility standards.

How quickly can Aerix install a hospital lift?

Aerix Elevators understands the urgency in healthcare settings. We have completed hospital lift installations in as little as 2–3 weeks for urgent requirements. Our project team coordinates closely with hospital management to minimize disruption.
