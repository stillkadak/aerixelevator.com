Goods Lift FAQs

What is the price of a goods lift in India?

Goods lift prices in India start from ₹5 Lakh for a basic 500kg hydraulic goods elevator. Heavy-duty 2-ton to 5-ton cargo elevators range from ₹8 Lakh to ₹20 Lakh depending on capacity, height, and structure. Contact Aerix for exact pricing.

What is the difference between hydraulic and traction goods lift?

Hydraulic goods lifts use a hydraulic pump and cylinder — ideal for low-rise (G+3 and below), heavy loads, and slow speeds. Traction goods lifts use a motor and ropes — better for high-rise, faster speeds, and energy efficiency. Aerix helps you choose the right type for your building.

Can goods lifts carry forklifts or vehicles?

Yes, Aerix Elevators manufactures platform lifts and vehicle lifts capable of carrying forklifts and automobiles. These are custom-engineered based on vehicle specifications. Contact us for industrial-grade solutions.
