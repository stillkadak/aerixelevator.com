FAQ

Home Lift FAQs

What is the cost of a home lift in Delhi in 2026?

Home lift prices in Delhi start from ₹3.5 Lakh for a basic 2-3 person residential elevator covering 3 floors. Premium glass cabin home lifts range from ₹5–8 Lakh depending on floors, capacity, and finish. Aerix Elevators offers free site inspection and exact quotes.

How much space does a home lift require?

Aerix home lifts require a minimum shaft size of 1200mm x 1200mm for a 2-person lift. Our compact designs have minimal pit depth requirements (as low as 100mm) and can fit into existing homes with minimal civil modification.

Are home lifts safe for elderly and children?

Yes, all Aerix home elevators include safety door sensors, overload protection, emergency stop, ARD (Automatic Rescue Device) in case of power failure, and intercom — making them completely safe for elderly family members and children.

Do I need a permit to install a home lift in Delhi?

For residential home lifts in independent villas and bungalows, generally no special permit is required from authorities in Delhi NCR. However, for multi-unit buildings or certain municipalities, approvals may be needed. Aerix Elevators guides you through the complete process.
